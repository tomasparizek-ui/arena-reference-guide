Camera placement tip: In Google Maps Photos, look for shots from the stands opposite the ice (central view), from corner seats, from above the goals, and from behind the nets. For recent shots click "Photos (1y)" — this filters images from the last year. The Wikipedia link opens the Slovak article, or a search if no specific article exists.
How to use: Click any link in an arena card — all open in a new tab. Google Maps has a "Photos" section in the left panel with dozens of fan photos (often including 360°). The image search query is pre-set for each arena with keywords for interior / ice surface.

For camera planning: Spiideo Camera Systems are typically installed at (1) center above the ice for full-pitch coverage, (2) hall corners for data and tracking, (3) behind the goals for review. When reviewing photos, look at: ceiling height, central stand availability, obstructions (skylights, scoreboard, rigging), and the structure behind the goals (netting, plexiglass, booths).
